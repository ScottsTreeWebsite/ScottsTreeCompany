Located in Wooster Ohio

Scott’s Tree Company is a small, local, family owned and operated business.
Scott’s Tree Company started in 2022 with only a few chainsaws,
a log splitter, and a dump truck. Since then we have been able to grow and
have gained a stump grinder, a bucket truck, a grapple truck and much
more to help aid us to provide the best service possible. We offer free quotes
for tree felling, removal, trimming, stump grinding, chipping, and more.
We thrive on learning and growing while getting to meet and service our local
community. Our men have over ten years of experience with wood cutting
and have enjoyed every minute of it. We guarantee to bring you a pleasant service
that will leave you bragging to your friends and families about your
tree’s for weeks. Scott's Tree Company is ready and willing to go out on a limb for you!
